Introduction
tl;dr: New to Google+? Looking for figures and people that post them? Try the Figure Photography community and #figurefriday tag. Also get notifications of the weekly posts.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'll be posting mostly about anime, manga or figures. There may be some deviations from that from time to time, but those will be the primary things I'll be posting about.

I circle many, but I usually follow just a handful of folks who post good content (as shown in my "People" section). The others are in muted topic circles that I frequent once in a while to see what's new.

So if I don't happen to see a post you made that you think would interest me, feel free to mention me :).

Hashtags:
These are some of the hashtags I like to take part in. Feel free to join in and participate!
  • Figure Findings (#figurefindings):
    • Hashtag to use when sharing photos taken by other people, links, information or discoveries.
  • Figure Photos (#figurephotos): page
    • Hashtag to use when posting your own photos of figures, nendroids, figmas and so on.
  • Figure Friday (#figurefriday): page, notification
    • We started this tag to encourage people to share their pictures on Fridays. You can read the announcement post for more details.
    • I'll usually pick a favorite from the previous week, so you can find that via that #figurefridayfavorite tag.
    • And we issue weekly challenges under the #figurefridaychallenge tag.
  • Miscellaneous
    • #morningdiscussion - Some topics of interest to discuss with people.
    • #everythingbecomesafigure - A collection of the strangest figures I've run across.
    • #shiritori - A word association game.
    • #notmyart - Sharing other people's artwork.
    • #yesnomaybedefinitely - Soliciting opinions on some titles.
